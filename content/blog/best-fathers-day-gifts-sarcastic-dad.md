Father's Day is sneaking up fast — and if your dad is the kind of person who communicates primarily in dry one-liners, raised eyebrows, and an impressive ability to say more with silence than most people say in paragraphs, this list is for him.

Forget the "#1 Dad" mugs. Here's what a sarcastic dad actually wants.

## The Rule: Match the Gift to the Humor Level

There are sarcastic dads, and then there are *sarcastic* dads. The first group chuckles at a funny card. The second group has an entire philosophy of life built around the idea that most things are funnier if you wait long enough.

The gifts below are for the second group.

## What Makes a Good Dad Gift, Actually

Here's the honest version: the best gifts for sarcastic dads share three traits.

They're useful. They say something true. And they don't try too hard.

A shirt that makes him laugh every time he puts it on hits all three. It's wearable. The message is real. And unlike a tie or a grilling spatula set, it has a point of view.
